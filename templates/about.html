This website is created with the intention of informing others of the most pressing current issues, including climate change, racial injustice, and identity-based discrimination, as well as providing viewers with resources to help minimize the negative impacts of such issues. It primarily focuses on issues in the United States, but other problems that reach international news will also be featured.

Each page contains a section for Informational Videos, Informational Readings, and Taking Action. If those sections are not present for a given page, please note that those sections are currently being updated. Thank you!
